About Me

My story

My name is Kelvin Odum, I am a Technology Management major, and a future front-end and web developer. I have a business mindset and technical knowledge in my background that enables me to design solutions that are practical and acceptable to users.

My specialties are the UI/UX, front-end development, and databases. I delight in creating clean, accessible, designs that are comfortable to the user and communicate the story of the brand behind the design.

With over 3 years in Technology Management, I've learned the importance of technology in organizations, business processes and problem-solving. I bring that same mindset into my projects — from designing webpages to working with tools like SAP, Excel, and Python.

I aspire to continue developing as a designer/developer, and creating tools that can simplify and enrich the lives of people.
