A little introduction about myself

My name is Kelvin. I am a student in Technology Management and a future front-end and web developer. I attend to learn the way design and technology intersect to generate purposeful, useful, and effectual digital experiences.

My research and accruing experience are developing my solid knowledge base in both business strategy and technical problem-solving. This assists me to come out with clarity, accessibility and creativity in the projects I carry out and ensure that they not only appear good but they also run smoothly.

I love helping people tell their story—through thoughtful layouts, calm typography, and subtle motion. Whether it’s a personal brand site, a product landing, or a simple tool, I strive to build with empathy and craft.
